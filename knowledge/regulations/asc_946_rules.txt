ASC 946 requires investment companies to disclose portfolio holdings in the Schedule of Investments.

Typical disclosures include:
- Issuer name
- Security description
- Principal amount or shares
- Market value
- Classification by investment type
- Footnote disclosures for restricted securities

The Schedule of Investments should be presented in a consistent order by category and issuer.

Investment companies must also disclose valuation methodology for securities categorized under Level 1, Level 2, or Level 3 of the fair value hierarchy.
