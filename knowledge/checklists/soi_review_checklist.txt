Schedule of Investments (SOI) Review Checklist

1. Security Classification
Verify each security is categorized under the correct asset class:
Equity, Corporate Bonds, Government Securities, CLO, ABS.

2. Sorting Order
Confirm securities are ordered consistently by category and issuer.

3. Duplicate Securities
Check for duplicate securities based on issuer + series + class.

4. Footnote Validation
Ensure restricted securities and Rule 144A securities include proper footnote references.

5. Floating Rate Disclosure
Floating rate securities referencing SOFR must include spread information.

6. Pricing Validation
Validate implied price = market value / par value.

7. Missing Attributes
Verify maturity date, coupon rate, issuer name, and value fields are present.

8. Prior Cycle Consistency
Compare positions with the previous reporting cycle.

9. Presentation Consistency
Ensure formatting and category ordering match prior financial statements.

10. Final Review
Confirm total investments reconcile with financial statement totals.
